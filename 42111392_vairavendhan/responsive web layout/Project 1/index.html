LONDON

London has four UNESCO world heritage sites: Tower of London, Maritime Greenwich, Westminster Palace and Kew's Royal Botanic Gardens. There are more than 22,000 music performances a year across London's 300+ venues including the O2 arena - the world's most popular music venue.

London boasts some of the oldest milliners in the world including Lock & Co (est. 1676) famous for creating Lord Nelson's original bicorn hat, as well as a specially commissioned version for Hatwalk as part of the London 2012 Festival.

London has played a major role in countless films from A Clockwork Orange to Harry Potter and is the world’s third busiest film production centre with over 14,000 ‘shooting days’ including the 24th Bond film Spectre, filmed here at City Hall.
